🎮 Arcade Games Instructions

📱 Mobile Note: All games work on mobile devices! Use touch controls and on-screen buttons.
🐍 Snake Game (5 tokens)
Cost: 5 tokens

Control a snake and eat food to grow longer. Don't hit the walls or yourself!

Controls:

🖱️ Desktop: Arrow keys to move
📱 Mobile: Swipe in direction to move

Goal: Eat as much food as possible without crashing!

🧩 Tetris (10 tokens)
Cost: 10 tokens

Stack falling blocks to create complete lines. Clear lines to score points!

Controls:

⬅️ Left Arrow: Move left
➡️ Right Arrow: Move right
⬇️ Down Arrow: Move down faster
⬆️ Up Arrow: Rotate piece
📱 Mobile: Tap screen edges to move, tap center to rotate

Goal: Clear as many lines as possible!

🧩 Number Puzzle (3 tokens)
Cost: 3 tokens

Slide tiles to arrange numbers 1-8 in order. The empty space is in the bottom right.

Controls:

🖱️ Desktop: Click tiles or use arrow keys
📱 Mobile: Tap tiles to move them

Goal: Arrange numbers 1-8 in order with empty space in bottom right!

🐦 Flappy Bird (4 tokens)
Cost: 4 tokens

Navigate through pipes by making the bird flap. Don't hit the pipes or ground!

Controls:

⌨️ Desktop: Press Spacebar to flap
📱 Mobile: Tap screen to flap

Goal: Fly through as many pipes as possible!

🏓 Breakout (6 tokens)
Cost: 6 tokens

Use your paddle to keep the ball in play and break all the blocks!

Controls:

🖱️ Desktop: Move mouse to control paddle
📱 Mobile: Touch and drag to move paddle

Goal: Break all blocks without losing the ball!

🏓 Pong (4 tokens)
Cost: 4 tokens

Classic two-player paddle game. You control the left paddle against AI!

Controls:

🖱️ Desktop: Move mouse up/down to control paddle
📱 Mobile: Touch and drag to move paddle

Goal: Don't let the ball pass your paddle!

☄️ Asteroids (5 tokens)
Cost: 5 tokens

Control a spaceship and destroy asteroids. Don't get hit!

Controls:

⬅️ Left Arrow: Rotate left
➡️ Right Arrow: Rotate right
⬆️ Up Arrow: Thrust forward
⌨️ Spacebar: Shoot
📱 Mobile: Use on-screen buttons

Goal: Destroy all asteroids without getting hit!

👻 Pac-Man (7 tokens)
Cost: 7 tokens

Navigate through the maze, eat dots, and avoid ghosts!

Controls:

⬅️ Left Arrow: Move left
➡️ Right Arrow: Move right
⬆️ Up Arrow: Move up
⬇️ Down Arrow: Move down
📱 Mobile: Swipe in direction to move

Goal: Eat all dots while avoiding ghosts!
